üé® Design Philosophy

Picasso √ó Afro-Realism combines the geometric fragmentation and bold colors of Cubism with the earthy warmth and pattern-rich aesthetics of African art traditions.

  • Geometric Shapes: Angular layouts and irregular border radii inspired by Cubist art
  • Earthy Palette: Terracotta, ochre, and deep browns from African landscapes
  • Bold Accents: Picasso-inspired blues, pinks, and yellows for energy
  • Kente Stripes: Colorful linear patterns in header accents
  • Hand-drawn Feel: Visible borders, dashed lines, and artistic shadows
  • Asymmetry: Slightly rotated elements for visual interest
